Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how London Octave collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information Collection

We may collect personal details such as your name and email address when you sign up for our newsletter or contact us via our website.

Use of Information

Your information is used solely to provide you with news about upcoming concerts and to process any enquiries or ticket purchases.

Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except where necessary for processing ticket sales (e.g., via TicketSource).

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you or to ask us to remove your data from our mailing list at any time.
