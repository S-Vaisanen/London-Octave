Prominent Players

London Octave is comprised of some of the finest musicians in the UK, many of whom hold principal positions in London's major orchestras.

LORRAINE McASLAN

leader, violin

Initially trained in London with David Martin, Lorraine McAslan moved to New York to study with Dorothy DeLay. She has since established herself as one of Britain's leading violinists, appearing as soloist with many of the world's major orchestras.

NORBERT BLUME

viola

Norbert Blume has been Principal Viola with the BBC Symphony Orchestra for over 20 years. He is a highly respected chamber musician and teacher.

DIETRICH BETHGE

director, cello

Founder and artistic director of London Octave, Dietrich Bethge has directed the ensemble from the cello in countless successful performances and recordings.

CHRIS WEST

double bass

Chris West is a prominent double bassist in the London music scene, known for his versatile playing in both orchestral and chamber settings.

CHRISTOPHER BEVAN

harpsichord, piano

Christopher Bevan is an accomplished keyboardist, specializing in period performance practice on harpsichord and fortepiano.

LONDON OCTAVE

A professional chamber orchestra dedicated to the finest performances of 17th and 18th-century repertoire.
